Our Mission

Certain communities across the United States have achieved the remarkable
feat of reducing euthanization of animals in their shelters to less than 10%.
We firmly believe that Atlanta has the potential to join these cities and become
a shining example of compassion and lifesaving efforts. To achieve this vision,
we are fully committed to advocating for homeless pet adoption, offering
affordable spay/neuter services, raising public awareness, and championing
policies that prioritize saving lives. Our goal is to transform a city with a
challenging history in animal welfare into a compassionate community with
a strong commitment to saving lives, ultimately serving as a model for the
entire nation. Our firm is actively working towards the ultimate objective of
turning metro Atlanta into a no-kill community that prioritizes the welfare
of its homeless pets.
